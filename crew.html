02Meet your crew

Commander

Douglas Hurley

Douglas Gerald Hurley is a United States Marine Corps officer and a NASA astronaut. He was a pilot on the Space Shuttle Atlantis and the last commander of the Space Shuttle program.

Mission Specialist

Mark Shuttleworth

Mark Richard Shuttleworth is the founder and CEO of Canonical, the company behind the Linux-based Ubuntu operating system. He became the first South African in space as a space tourist.
